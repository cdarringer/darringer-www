   About The Algorithm

   The Darringer AI Equity Market Model is an artificial neural network written and tested in Python. The model looks at several factors such as profits, P/E, EBITDA, and other information given in quarterly reports. This data is then coupled with macro-economic data, in which the neural network decides which sectors/industries will perform best given the probable near term economic circumstances. You can contact us if you have further questions about our algorithm.
